Newest Car: Tesla Model S Plaid

About the Tesla Model S Plaid

The Tesla Model S Plaid is the latest innovation in electric vehicles, offering groundbreaking performance, luxurious design, and cutting-edge technology. Known for its unparalleled acceleration, reaching 0-60 mph in under 2 seconds, it has set a new standard for electric vehicles worldwide.

Key Features

  • Top speed of 200 mph
  • Range of up to 396 miles on a single charge
  • Three-motor all-wheel-drive system
  • 17-inch cinematic display with a gaming computer
  • Full self-driving capabilities (available as an option)
  • Luxurious interior with ventilated seats

Specifications

  • Battery: 100 kWh lithium-ion
  • Horsepower: Over 1,000 HP
  • Torque: Instant electric torque
  • Charging: Supercharging network compatible
  • Wheels: 19" or 21" aluminum-alloy wheels
  • Weight: 4,766 lbs

Additional Information

The Tesla Model S Plaid is designed for drivers seeking peak performance, with industry-leading aerodynamics and advanced thermal management systems. Tesla continues to redefine what an electric car can achieve by blending efficiency and power in one sleek package.

With over-the-air software updates, the Model S Plaid ensures that your car gets better with time, delivering new features and optimizations without needing a visit to the service center.
